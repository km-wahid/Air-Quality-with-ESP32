Did You Know?

Air pollution is responsible for approximately 7 million premature deaths worldwide each year. Monitoring air quality helps us make informed decisions about our outdoor activities.

Indoor Air Quality

Indoor air can be 2-5 times more polluted than outdoor air. Regular ventilation, air purifiers, and houseplants can significantly improve your indoor environment.

Health Impact

Poor air quality affects respiratory and cardiovascular health. Children, elderly, and those with pre-existing conditions are most vulnerable. Stay informed and take precautions on high pollution days.

What You Can Do
  • Use public transportation or carpool
  • Conserve energy at home and work
  • Plant trees and support green spaces
  • Avoid burning wood or trash
  • Monitor air quality before outdoor activities
